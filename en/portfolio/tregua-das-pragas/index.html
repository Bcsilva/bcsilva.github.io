The Truce of Pests

  • Year: 2017
  • Work: Illustration / Experimental
  • Intermediate: 2017 illustration contest from Folha de São Paulo

Experimental work made for 2017 edition of illustration contest from Folha de São Paulo newspaper.

These arts consists of a series of illustrations with ironic situations between humans and pests.

The idea was satirize plagues (cockroaches, rats, spiders, etc) making them help the human society for trivial things.

No more waste!
After all, they eat the same food that is out of people’s mouths.

Cleaning!
Enough of complaining of garbagemen absence!

Security!
Less expenses with failed systems!

Gratitude!
For all years being hunted like witches!
